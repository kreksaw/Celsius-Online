Cross-platform Web & Mobile MMO

Renaissance Kingdoms

Translated into 26 languages, Renaissance Kingdoms is Europe's leading cross-platform Web & Mobile MMO role-playing and management game. In this persistent simulation of 15th-century Europe, you play a character taking part in the economic, political and social life of your village, county and kingdom.

21+ years live · LiveOps activity as intense as ever
